The main idea behind this is the hiding of implementation details from users. 
> In other words, Encapsulation is the wrapping up of data into a single unit in order to prevent access to it from the outside world.
> Since the data is hidden from the other classes, this process is also known as Data-hiding.


Encapsulation acheived by = Data hiding + abstraction .

How Encapsulation work ?
> By hiding all data members 
> making methods accessble outside the class (usually with some validation ) - by convention getter and setter methods are used
